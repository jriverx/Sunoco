Virginia Inspection Requirements

Virginia law requires annual safety inspections and emissions testing to keep your vehicle properly registered.

  • Safety Inspection: every year.
  • Emissions Test: every 2 years (after your vehicle's 4th year).
  • If your vehicle does not pass, our certified technicians diagnose and repair the issue efficiently.
